Price Action Strategies

Pin Bar

A Pin Bar is a single candlestick pattern that indicates a potential reversal in price direction. It has a long tail (or wick) that points in the opposite direction of the expected price movement.

Key Characteristics:

  • Long tail (wick)
  • Small body
  • Little or no upper wick (for bearish Pin Bar) or lower wick (for bullish Pin Bar)

Engulfing Pattern

An Engulfing Pattern is a two-candlestick pattern that signals a potential reversal. The second candlestick completely "engulfs" the body of the first candlestick.

Key Characteristics:

  • Two candlesticks
  • The second candlestick's body completely covers the first candlestick's body
  • The pattern is more significant if it occurs after a clear uptrend or downtrend
